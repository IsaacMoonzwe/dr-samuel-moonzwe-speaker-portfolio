Speaker Profile

Dr. Samuel “Dr. Sam” Moonzwe serves as a mission strategist and Bible teacher through Empowerment Ministries International (EMI), equipping churches and leaders for faithful ministry and Gospel engagement.

Dr. and Mrs. Moonzwe—founders of EMI and parents of three—bring uncommon depth in scholarship, ministry, and executive leadership. Dr. Moonzwe holds a Doctorate in Missiology and master’s degrees in Theology, Intercultural Studies, Business Administration, and Management.

His career spans senior leadership, including service as CEO of a major Zambian corporation. Studied in Africa, Europe, and the USA, he unites boardroom rigor with frontline mission experience—helping leaders translate biblical conviction into practical mission impact.

He also serves in Bakersfield, California, helping lead the Missions Department and the School of Ministry—supporting the local church in raising disciples and sending workers into the harvest.

EMI Programs

These programs reflect EMI’s focus: strengthening the church, sharing the Gospel, and serving communities with practical care that honors Christ.

Pastoral Training & Church Planting

Equipping pastors and leaders with biblical depth, ministry skill, and healthy leadership practices to strengthen churches and support sustainable church planting.

Street & Rural Evangelism

Gospel proclamation in public spaces and remote communities—supporting local believers, cultivating discipleship, and strengthening community witness.

Girls Rescue Dorm Project

Protective housing that supports continued education and safety—helping vulnerable girls remain in school and grow in stability and hope.

Community Development

Practical support initiatives including clean water access and community uplift—meeting needs while strengthening the church’s local presence.
