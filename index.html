About Empowerment Ministries International (EMI)

Empowerment Ministries International (EMI) partners with local churches to strengthen biblical leadership, deepen missions engagement, and equip the Body of Christ through teaching rooted in Scripture and lived global ministry.

EMI is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization in the State of California, serving churches across the United States and internationally.

Speaker Profile

Dr. Samuel “Dr. Sam” Moonzwe is the co-founder and director of Empowerment Ministries International (EMI), a Christ-centered ministry committed to strengthening churches and advancing gospel engagement across Africa and the global Church.

He holds a Doctorate in Missiology and multiple master’s degrees in Theology, Intercultural Studies, Business Administration, and Management. He also serves faithfully in Bakersfield, California, helping lead the Missions Department and the School of Ministry.

His background includes senior executive leadership, including service as CEO of a major Zambian corporation—uniting boardroom rigor with frontline mission experience.
